The Free University in Malawi

We believe education is a right. That's why we're developing a project to make it more affordable and accessible for everyone.

Our project aims to provide high-quality education to students from all backgrounds. We'll be using scholarships, grants, flexible payment plans, and partnerships to achieve this.

We need partners, donors, and supporters to make this happen. If you're passionate about education and want to make a difference, please contact us.

Together, we can break down barriers and give students the opportunity to succeed. Contact Mr. Chipiliro Mizere on WhatsApp at 0996982449 or via email at chipiliromizere@icloud.com to join us.
